easyDash App

easyDash App (Nominated for Werner Von Siemens Award 2021)

Customers were interested in connecting and monitoring their assets with MindSphere (The Siemens Industrial IoT cloud platform, now rebranded to Insights Hub), but struggled as other existing offerings were either too expensive, too complex, or not powerful enough. easyDash brings Grafana into MindSphere and is an easy-to-use dashboarding application that allows anyone to create no-code, attractive, insightful dashboards within minutes. It addresses the customer demand for a simple, affordable, and powerful dashboarding tool that offers great flexibility as a starting point for industrial IoT. The application reached 100+ customers globally within 9 months including large enterprise customers, SMEs, and also Siemens factories. Siemens presales teams around the world are using easyDash to sell Siemens IoT solutions. It has been shown to decrease their sales cycles from 6-9 months to as short as 3 months. Siemens HQ decided to purchase the rights to the application from Australia to further integrate its functionalities into MindSphere. Find out more about the app here.

Dulux Process Digital Twin

Dulux Process Digital Twin

This image might not look like much. However, the 4 industrial PCs are simulating the entire control system of the Dulux Merrifield production plant. I was part of the team that delivered a simulation of the full production controller hardware as well as its interface for the Dulux Merrifield factory. The simulation was done using SIMIT, a Siemens software. Having virtualised hardware allowed the development team to develop the control software on the actual controller hardware while the testing team tested the updates on the simulation platform. This not only cut software development time by at least 8 weeks, but the simulation was also used for the onboarding of new plant employees who would learn the operation on the simulation before working on the actual factory.
